Spout

Port of the "Spout" game by kuni to SDL2 and Emscripten.

Controls:

  • Arrows and 4, 6, 8, 2 on Keypad – Movement.
  • Z, Space, Return and 5 on Keypad – Fire.
  • Q – Pause.
  • Q + W or E – Reset Game.
